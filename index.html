Gem Collectors Game


Welcome to the Gem Collectors game!

Click the "Start New Game" to activate the game and you will be given a target number in the right hand panel.

When you click the gem stones, the current number counter will increase. Each gem will increase your current total by a different value. For example the amethyst may increase your total by 5, but the ruby may increase the value by 9.

To win, collect the correct amount of gems to match the target number. When you exceed the target number, you lose the game. To play a new game, simply click the button above.

For each new game the values will reset and you will be given a new target number. The value for each gem will also update as well. So click wisely!
